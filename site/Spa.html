HANDS, FEET & WAXING

MEDITATION CLASSES

As people continue to strive for the perfect work-life balance, our Spa is offering
complimentary daily 20-minute mindfulness meditation classes over lunchtime to
raise awareness of the benefits of coping with stress and anxiety in the workplace.

Mindfulness meditation is an adaptation of Buddhist meditation, which encourages
one to focus on the moment rather than being consumed by the pain of the past or
anxiety over the future. Spa therapists have been practising daily meditation since
the launch of our Spa in 2009 to find personal balance, as it also helps them sense
and craft unique and tailored treatments for our guests.

Guests are welcomed in the lobby by a spa representative and ushered to the spa
into a cool and dimly-lit treatment room where they are asked to remove their shoes
and turn off any technological equipment. René Van Eyssen, our Spa Director, explains,
“Meditation encourages you to observe your emotions, which improves awareness of
the present moment, bringing clarity and peace. The result is a total renewal of mind and
spirit.”
