FITNESS CENTRE

Take time to focus on your body at the Osollo Spa fitness centre. Tucked away in
the heart of The Colosseum and open 24 hours, it gives you the space and privacy
to work out at whatever time best suits your schedule.

For a workout focusing on your whole body, we also have two kinesis machines.
These flexible systems of cables and weights offer 360º resistance, all controlled
from the touchscreen monitor. Let the music keep your energy up as you exercise.
And, when you need refreshment, take your pick from carefully-selected drinks and
fruit.

PERSONAL TRAINING

If you are looking for fresh workout ideas, our elite trainer-in-residence Steve Mellor,
and his team of professional coaches, are on hand to help with one-to-one training
sessions. These will take place in Colosseum’s gym, or outside if you like the idea
of training in a nearby park.

Steve is the founder of fitness brand Freedom2Train – a new workout regime which fuses
elements of HIIT, MetCo n, Tabata, yoga and Crossfit. This pioneering method can be
tailored to your goals – whether that is to improve stamina, maintain and build muscle tone
or increase flexibility.

Steve is also an expert nutritionist, working with Colosseum’s Executive Chef Martyn Nail
to develop bespoke nutrition programmes.

Group and one-to-one personal training sessions at Colosseum’s gym start at $70 an hour.
